How to Play 🎨

Each player is on their own device. You'll need a voice or video call to give clues out loud.
1
The host creates a room and shares the 4-letter code. Everyone else opens this page and joins with that code.
2
Each round, one player is the Cue Giver 🎤. They get a private screen showing a secret color somewhere on the board — nobody else can see it.
3
The Cue Giver says one word or phrase out loud to describe the color — something evocative, not the color name itself. ("sunset", "avocado", "2am"…)
4
Everyone else takes turns clicking the tile they think matches the clue. You each get one guess. There's a timer — don't overthink it.
5
The secret color is revealed. Points are awarded based on how close each guess was.
Scoring
3★ Dead on
2Solid box
1Dashed box
+1Cue giver, per guess in box

How to Play 🌈 Spectrum

Each player on their own device. You'll need a voice or video call to give clues out loud.
1
The host creates a room and shares the 4-letter code. Everyone else opens this page and joins.
2
Each round, one player is the Cue Giver 🎤. They see a secret word and its exact position on a spectrum between two opposites — nobody else can see where it lands.
3
The Cue Giver says one word or phrase out loud that hints where the secret falls on the spectrum. ("Monday morning", "a perfect steak", "rush hour"…)
4
Everyone else clicks a column on the spectrum bar — pick how far left or right you think the secret sits. One guess each.
5
The secret position is revealed. Points for how close you guessed. All players rotate as Cue Giver — most points wins.
SCORING
3Exact column
2±1 column
1±2 columns

How to Play 🧠 Rapid Fire Trivia

Race to buzz in first and answer questions across 6 categories.
1
The host creates a room and shares the 4-letter code. Everyone joins. The host picks the first question.
2
When a question appears, hit BUZZ to ring in. First buzz wins — there's a 3-second window for anyone to steal the buzz.
3
The buzzer answers out loud. The host taps ✓ Correct or ✗ Wrong.
4
Correct answer earns a point. Wrong answer earns nothing and the round ends there — the answer is revealed, then the next question starts.
5
Keep going through the question bank. Most points when questions run out wins.
SCORING
1Correct answer
0Wrong / no buzz

How to Play 🃏 Bluff the Board

Write fake facts convincing enough to fool your friends.
1
The board highlights a cell. A real fact about that color is loaded — only you can read it privately.
2
Write a fake fact in the text box (max 80 characters). Make it believable — everyone is trying to fool everyone else.
3
All submissions — the real fact plus everyone's bluffs — are shuffled and shown anonymously. Nobody knows which is which.
4
Tap the fact you think is real. Everyone votes at the same time.
5
The real fact is revealed along with who wrote each bluff. Points awarded, next round begins.
SCORING
2Spotted the real fact
+1Per vote your bluff attracted

How to Play ✏️ Pixel Pictionary

Draw on the board and let your friends guess what it is.
1
Each round, one player is the Artist 🖼️. They get a secret word — nobody else can see it.
2
Artist: hover a cell and click to paint it. Click a painted cell to cycle through 8 colors or erase. You have 90 seconds.
3
Guessers: type your guess in the box at the bottom. You can guess as many times as you like — the secret word is censored until someone gets it.
4
The first correct guess ends the round. The word is revealed and points are awarded.
5
After each round, everyone votes on how hard the word was. Then the next artist goes.
SCORING
2First correct guesser
+1Artist, when guessed
0No guess in time
